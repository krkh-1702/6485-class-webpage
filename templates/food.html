Nutritional Equity Detector is using the YouOnlyLookOnce v4. We propose to intervene in nutritional inequalities that hinge on key sociodemographic factors, such as geographic location, age, gender, ethnicity, education, and wealth. The project targets avoidable situations by shifting the information asymmetry regarding food nutrition by giving personalized feedback from NED, raising awareness of dietary balance.
